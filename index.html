A SLAVE SET FREE

I am a slave set free,
shall i not wander and be lost
For all out there remains still,
while you are strangled on the Hill,
The hill of hopes and expectations,
Not your own but too fickle and frown.
But I am free,
as I have fled,
the ayslum of ghoul,
to seek refuge in soul.

The breeze bussing my cheeks,
the grass feeling my feet.
Shall i no wander and be lost,
For I have caught what you've fled.
For I exist and you not.
though lost in my thoughts,
you don't retreat form your lots.
The anguish will dawn, and the suffering will reign,
on you who is always in vain.
For you care about the world's disdain.

For the words of criticism stab you in vein.
The robe you wear, the shoe you shine,
the wine you drink, the song you rhyme,
will not be there,its just a matter of time.
Shall you not set thyself free,
fly away from pretence, and you shall flee,
noise of the vice,
as I have fled,
and will you be free.
to seek refuge in soul.
